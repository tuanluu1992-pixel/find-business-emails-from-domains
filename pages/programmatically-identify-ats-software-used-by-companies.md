# Programmatically Identify Applicant Tracking System Software Used by Target Companies

Knowing which ATS a company uses is critical for recruitment automation and sales intelligence workflows. Rather than manually visiting career pages or guessing, you can automate ATS detection by analyzing the technical signatures, URL patterns, and HTML structure of a company's careers portal.

The [ATS Board Detector actor](https://apify.com/qualifyops/ats-board-detector) scans company career pages and instantly identifies whether they're powered by Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, Workable, SmartRecruiters, or other major platforms. This saves hours of research and enables you to build targeted recruitment outreach campaigns, integrate directly with the correct job board API, or understand competitor hiring infrastructure.

Run it against a list of target companies to systematically map their ATS implementations. Perfect for recruitment agencies, talent acquisition teams, and business intelligence professionals who need structured data on hiring technology stacks at scale.
