# Enrich Prospect Lists with Website Qualification and Decision-Maker Intelligence

Building a sales pipeline requires more than just a list of company names—you need verified contact information for the actual decision-makers who can say yes. Manual research wastes hours; automated enrichment scales your outreach.

The [Website Lead Qualifier & Contact Finder](https://apify.com/qualifyops/website-lead-qualifier-contact-finder) analyzes company websites to extract business intelligence, verify decision-maker details, and qualify prospects based on hiring signals, technology stack, and firmographic data. It processes multiple domains simultaneously, identifying key contacts across departments—sales leaders, operations managers, founders—with validated email addresses and job titles.

This approach eliminates guesswork. Instead of cold-calling generic info@ addresses, you reach verified stakeholders with personalized context about their company's tech choices and growth signals. Results sync directly to your CRM for immediate outreach.
