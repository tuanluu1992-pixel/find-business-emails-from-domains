# Monitor Companies Hiring Multiple Positions Simultaneously

Identifying when companies post multiple job openings in short timeframes reveals expansion plans, budget increases, and strategic growth initiatives—valuable signals for sales, recruitment, and business intelligence teams.

Instead of manually checking career pages daily, the [ATS Hiring Surge Monitor](https://apify.com/qualifyops/ats-hiring-surge-monitor) automatically detects when target companies rapidly increase hiring velocity across their applicant tracking systems. The actor monitors Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, and other major ATS platforms, triggering alerts when surge patterns emerge.

This workflow captures hiring intent data in real-time, enabling sales teams to reach prospects during growth phases when budgets unlock and decision-makers prioritize vendor evaluation. Recruitment teams gain early visibility into competitor talent acquisition strategies, while market researchers track industry hiring trends by sector and geography.

Run the actor on a scheduled interval to build historical hiring surge datasets, correlate patterns with earnings calls or funding announcements, and identify your highest-probability prospects before competitors notice.
