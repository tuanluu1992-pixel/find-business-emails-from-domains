# How to Scrape Job Listings from Greenhouse ATS Automatically

Greenhouse is a popular applicant tracking system used by thousands of companies to manage job postings. Manually checking career pages for new positions is time-consuming and inefficient.

You can automate job data extraction using the [ATS Jobs Scraper](https://apify.com/qualifyops/ats-jobs-scraper) actor, which handles Greenhouse, Lever, and Ashby platforms. The scraper extracts job titles, descriptions, requirements, locations, and application links directly from ATS career pages.

Simply provide the company's career page URL, and the actor will parse all open positions and return structured JSON data. This is ideal for job aggregation platforms, recruitment research, market analysis, or building targeted candidate pipelines. The automation runs in minutes and outputs clean, ready-to-use job data without manual effort or delays.
