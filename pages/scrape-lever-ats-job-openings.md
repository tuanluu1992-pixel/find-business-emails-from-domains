# Scrape Job Openings from Lever ATS Automatically

Lever is a popular applicant tracking system used by thousands of companies for recruitment. Manually checking each career page for new job postings is time-consuming and inefficient.

The [ATS Jobs Scraper](https://apify.com/qualifyops/ats-jobs-scraper) extracts job listings directly from Lever-powered career pages, capturing job titles, descriptions, departments, experience levels, and application links. This automation works across multiple job boards and ATS platforms including Greenhouse and Ashby.

Use this data to monitor hiring trends, identify growing companies, track new roles, or feed qualified candidates into your recruitment pipeline. The scraper returns structured data ready for analysis or integration into your tools.

Schedule regular scraping runs to stay updated on new openings without manual effort.
