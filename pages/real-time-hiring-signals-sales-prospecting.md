# Detect Real-Time Hiring Signals for Sales Prospecting

Identifying companies with active hiring needs is crucial for B2B sales teams. Instead of manually checking dozens of careers pages, you can automate this discovery process.

The [ATS Hiring Signal Finder](https://apify.com/qualifyops/ats-hiring-signal-finder) actor monitors applicant tracking systems and career pages to detect when companies are actively recruiting. It analyzes hiring patterns, job posting volume, and ATS platform changes to surface genuine hiring intent signals.

This approach helps you prioritize outreach to companies genuinely expanding their teams right now, rather than reaching out to dormant job boards. The actor processes hiring data systematically, giving your sales team actionable leads with confirmed hiring activity. You get a list of companies with verified recruitment momentum, enabling more efficient pipeline building without guesswork about hiring needs.
