# Scrape Jobs from Ashby ATS Careers Page Automatically

Ashby is a modern applicant tracking system used by fast-growing startups and scale-ups. If you need to monitor job openings across multiple Ashby-powered careers pages, automated scraping saves hours of manual checking.

You can schedule recurring scrapes to track new openings over time, identify hiring trends, or build a database of active positions across target companies.
