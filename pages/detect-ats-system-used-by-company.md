# Detect Which ATS a Company Uses

Identifying a company's applicant tracking system (ATS) is crucial for recruitment teams, job board operators, and HR professionals who need to understand hiring infrastructure. Manual checking of career pages and job postings is time-consuming and often unreliable.

The [ATS Board Detector](https://apify.com/qualifyops/ats-board-detector) automatically analyzes company career pages and identifies which ATS platform they're using—whether it's Greenhouse, Lever, Workable, Ashby, or other major systems. This saves hours of research and provides accurate data for integration planning, competitive intelligence, and recruitment strategy optimization.

Simply input a company's career page URL or domain, and the actor detects the underlying ATS with a confidence score. Perfect for building recruitment databases, vendor evaluations, or understanding competitor hiring workflows at scale.
