# Find Business Email Addresses by Domain Name

Need to build a contact list for a specific company domain? Finding accurate business emails directly from a domain is faster than manual research and more reliable than guessing formats.

The [Website Contact Enrichment actor](https://apify.com/qualifyops/website-contact-enrichment) automatically discovers and verifies business email addresses associated with any domain. It crawls company websites, extracts contact information from multiple sources (footer links, contact pages, team directories), and validates each email to ensure deliverability.

This approach saves hours of manual searching and significantly improves your outreach accuracy. You get verified, domain-authenticated emails ready for legitimate B2B communication, sales prospecting, or partnership inquiries. Simply input your target domain and receive a structured dataset of validated business contacts with confidence scores.
