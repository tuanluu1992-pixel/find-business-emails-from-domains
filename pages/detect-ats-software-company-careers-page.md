# Detect ATS Software Used by Companies Automatically

Identifying which applicant tracking system (ATS) a company uses is crucial for sales teams, recruiters, and HR professionals targeting hiring decision-makers. Rather than manually checking each careers page, you can automate this detection process.

The [ATS Board Detector actor](https://apify.com/qualifyops/ats-board-detector) analyzes company careers pages and identifies the underlying ATS platform—whether it's Workday, Greenhouse, Lever, BambooHR, or dozens of other systems. This intelligence helps you understand a company's hiring infrastructure and tailor your outreach accordingly.

By automating ATS detection across multiple companies, you can build targeted prospect lists of organizations using specific platforms, prioritize accounts based on their hiring tech stack, and personalize your pitch to hiring teams. This approach is far more efficient than manual research and provides actionable data for sales and recruitment strategies.
