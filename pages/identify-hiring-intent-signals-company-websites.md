# Identify Hiring Intent Signals from Company Websites

Detecting when companies are actively recruiting requires analyzing multiple signals beyond just job postings. Hiring intent indicators include career page updates, new job board listings, LinkedIn hiring announcements, and ATS system changes.

The [ATS Hiring Signal Finder](https://apify.com/qualifyops/ats-hiring-signal-finder) automatically scans company websites and identifies technical hiring signals by detecting applicant tracking systems, career page activity, and recruitment infrastructure. This helps you pinpoint companies in active hiring mode before they're flooded with applications.

Instead of manually checking each competitor or prospect, this actor extracts signals indicating genuine hiring intent—perfect for sales prospecting, competitor analysis, or recruitment intelligence. You get structured data on which companies are genuinely hiring versus those with static job pages, enabling smarter outreach timing and higher conversion rates.
