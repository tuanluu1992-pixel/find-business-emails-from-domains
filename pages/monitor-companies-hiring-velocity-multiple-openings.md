# Monitor Companies with Multiple Job Openings and Hiring Velocity

Identifying companies experiencing rapid hiring expansion helps sales teams spot growth signals and expansion timelines. Rather than tracking isolated job postings, you need to measure *hiring velocity*—how quickly a company is posting new positions across departments.

The [ATS Hiring Surge Monitor](https://apify.com/qualifyops/ats-hiring-monitor) automatically detects when companies post multiple job openings within short timeframes. It identifies hiring surges by monitoring Greenhouse, Lever, and Ashby career pages, measuring posting frequency patterns that indicate expansion or scaling phases.

This approach beats manual tracking because it captures hiring momentum in real-time, showing you which companies are actively recruiting across multiple roles. You'll discover expansion plans before competitors notice, enabling early outreach to expanding organizations during their growth phase when decision-makers actively evaluate new tools and vendors.

Key value: Automated velocity detection surfaces genuine expansion signals, not just individual job posts.
