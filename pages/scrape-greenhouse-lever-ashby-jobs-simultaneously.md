# Scrape Jobs from Multiple ATS Platforms at Once

Need to monitor job openings across Greenhouse, Lever, and Ashby careers pages? Manual checking wastes hours. The [ATS Jobs Scraper](https://apify.com/qualifyops/ats-jobs-scraper) extracts job listings, titles, descriptions, and application links from all three platforms automatically.

Run it on a schedule to track new positions in real-time without visiting each careers page individually. Perfect for recruiting teams identifying high-growth companies, sales teams spotting hiring signals, or researchers analyzing job market trends.

The actor handles pagination, dynamic content, and employment details extraction—saving you days of manual work monthly.
