# Extract Email Addresses from Company About Us Pages Automatically

Company About Us pages often contain verified contact information for founders, executives, and team members that isn't listed on careers pages. Manually collecting these emails is tedious and error-prone.

Instead of copying emails one-by-one, you can automate the extraction process. A specialized web scraper can visit target company About Us pages, identify email addresses embedded in text, author bios, and footer links, then verify them against the company domain.

This approach captures decision-makers who may not be posting jobs but are still relevant contacts—perfect for relationship-building outreach. The [Website Contact Enrichment actor](https://apify.com/qualifyops/website-contact-enrichment) automatically extracts and validates business emails from About Us pages and other key website sections, enriching your prospect database with verified decision-maker contacts in minutes instead of hours.
