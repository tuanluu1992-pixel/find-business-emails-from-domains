# Scrape Job Postings from Greenhouse, Lever & Ashby Careers Pages

Extract structured job posting data directly from Greenhouse, Lever, and Ashby applicant tracking systems without building custom scrapers. This approach captures job titles, descriptions, requirements, compensation, and application URLs in a single workflow.

The [ATS Jobs Scraper](https://apify.com/qualifyops/ats-jobs-scraper) automates extraction from multiple ATS platforms simultaneously, saving weeks of development time. Input any careers page URL and receive clean, structured JSON with all job details, posting dates, and hiring manager information.

Use this data for competitive hiring analysis, recruitment benchmarking, or building job aggregation tools. The actor handles pagination automatically and exports results to CSV, JSON, or directly to your database for real-time job market intelligence.
