# Detect Hiring Signals From Company Career Pages Automatically

Identifying companies with genuine hiring intent requires analyzing real signals beyond job board postings. The [ATS Hiring Signal Finder](https://apify.com/qualifyops/ats-hiring-signal-finder) automatically detects when companies are actively recruiting by scanning their career pages for hiring momentum indicators—such as multiple open positions, posting frequency, and growth patterns.

This approach gives you a competitive advantage in sales prospecting by revealing companies in active hiring mode before they announce layoffs or slowdowns. Rather than relying on outdated job boards, you get real-time signals directly from company websites where recruitment decisions actually happen.

The actor works across different ATS platforms (Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, and custom solutions), capturing hiring velocity and workforce expansion trends. Perfect for B2B sales teams targeting high-intent prospects who are demonstrably investing in growth and hiring new talent right now.
