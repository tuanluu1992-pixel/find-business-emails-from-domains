# Monitor Company Hiring Activity & Detect Recruitment Trends

Tracking hiring surges helps you identify growth signals, market opportunities, and competitive movements before they become common knowledge. When companies rapidly expand their teams, it indicates funding rounds, market expansion, or product launches.

The [ATS Hiring Surge Monitor](https://apify.com/qualifyops/ats-hiring-surge-monitor) automatically detects when target companies post multiple job openings within specific timeframes. It monitors career pages across different applicant tracking systems, tracking hiring velocity and volume changes in real-time.

This actor captures hiring signals including position count increases, department expansion patterns, and recruitment timeline shifts. You get structured data showing which companies are actively recruiting, how many positions opened, departments affected, and salary ranges when available.

Perfect for sales teams identifying expansion-stage prospects, recruiters finding growth companies, or competitive intelligence gathering. Set monitoring intervals to catch hiring surges as they happen, enabling faster outreach to decision-makers during critical growth moments when budgets unlock and priorities shift.
