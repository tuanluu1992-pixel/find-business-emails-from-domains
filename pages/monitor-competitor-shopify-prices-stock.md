# Monitor Competitor Shopify Prices & Stock Levels Automatically

Tracking competitor pricing and inventory changes manually is time-consuming and error-prone. The [Shopify Competitor Price Monitor actor](https://apify.com/qualifyops/shopify-competitor-price-monitor) automates this process by continuously scraping your competitors' Shopify stores.

This tool extracts real-time product prices, stock availability, and inventory changes across multiple competitor stores. You'll receive structured data showing price fluctuations, out-of-stock alerts, and product catalog updates—perfect for dynamic pricing strategies and competitive analysis.

Run it on a schedule to build historical price databases, identify market trends, and react faster to competitor moves. The actor handles pagination and product variations automatically, saving hours of manual monitoring weekly.
