# Track Shopify Competitor Inventory Changes Automatically

Monitoring when competitors' inventory levels shift reveals strategic opportunities—stock-outs signal demand spikes, while overstock indicates clearance pricing coming. Manual tracking wastes hours daily.

The [Shopify Competitor Price Monitor](https://apify.com/qualifyops/shopify-competitor-price-monitor) automates this entirely. It continuously scrapes competitor Shopify stores, capturing real-time price fluctuations and stock availability across product catalogs. You'll instantly spot when rivals adjust pricing, launch promotions, or deplete inventory—giving you competitive intelligence to adjust your own strategy.

Set up scheduled runs to track changes over time. Export data to your business tools for analysis. Stay ahead of market movements without manual work.
