# Qualify B2B Leads & Extract Decision-Maker Contacts Automatically

Building a sales pipeline requires more than just contact lists—you need verified decision-makers at companies that match your ideal customer profile. Manual research wastes hours on unqualified prospects.

The [Website Lead Qualifier & Contact Finder](https://apify.com/qualifyops/website-lead-qualifier-contact-finder) actor automatically analyzes websites to assess business quality, extract verified decision-maker contacts, and qualify leads based on your criteria. It combines website intelligence with contact discovery, reducing your research time from days to minutes.

This approach works across industries: SaaS founders can target growing tech companies, agencies can identify businesses with outdated websites, and recruiters can find hiring managers at target organizations. The actor validates contact information and surface-level business signals to help you focus outreach on prospects most likely to convert.

Use this for initial qualification before personalized outreach, not for bulk mass-contact campaigns.
