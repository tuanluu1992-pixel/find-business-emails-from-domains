# Validate Business Email Addresses for Domain Verification

Verifying that business email addresses actually belong to a specific domain is crucial for B2B outreach and lead qualification. Manual checks are time-consuming and error-prone, especially when working with large prospect lists.

The [Website Contact Enrichment actor](https://apify.com/qualifyops/website-contact-enrichment) automates email validation by confirming whether addresses are legitimate contacts associated with target domains. It cross-references emails against company websites and public records to ensure deliverability and authenticity before you invest in outreach campaigns.

This approach helps you:
- Reduce bounce rates in cold email sequences
- Build more accurate contact databases
- Verify decision-maker legitimacy before engagement
- Filter out fake or outdated email addresses
- Improve campaign ROI by targeting verified contacts only

Automate your email validation workflow to focus outreach efforts on genuine, reachable business contacts.
