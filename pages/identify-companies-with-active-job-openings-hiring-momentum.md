# Identify Companies with Active Job Openings and Hiring Momentum

Finding companies in active hiring mode is crucial for sales teams targeting growth-stage businesses and recruiters seeking engaged employers. Instead of manually checking career pages, use the [ATS Hiring Signal Finder](https://apify.com/qualifyops/ats-hiring-signal-finder) to automatically detect companies posting multiple job openings across their applicant tracking systems.

This actor monitors career pages and identifies hiring velocity—companies rapidly filling positions signal expansion, budget availability, and decision-maker accessibility. The tool analyzes Greenhouse, Lever, and Ashby systems simultaneously, capturing hiring patterns that indicate immediate business needs.

By automating hiring intent detection, you focus outreach on companies most likely to engage with sales conversations, reducing wasted prospecting efforts. The actor delivers real-time signals, ensuring your team contacts hiring managers during their most receptive window when they're actively recruiting and evaluating new vendors.
