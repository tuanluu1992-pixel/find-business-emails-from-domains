# Find Companies Actively Hiring Right Now

Identifying companies with immediate hiring needs saves time in recruitment and sales outreach. Rather than manually tracking job postings across multiple platforms, you can automate the discovery of hiring signals.

The [ATS Hiring Signal Finder](https://apify.com/qualifyops/ats-hiring-signal-finder) actor analyzes applicant tracking system data to detect when companies are actively recruiting. It identifies hiring intent patterns by monitoring job postings, application volumes, and recruitment activity—giving you real-time visibility into companies expanding their teams.

This approach works by extracting signals from public ATS platforms that indicate genuine hiring urgency, not just theoretical openings. You'll know which companies are actually interviewing candidates and building teams right now, making your outreach more timely and relevant.
