# Track Companies with Hiring Surges Automatically

Identifying companies experiencing rapid hiring growth is valuable for sales prospecting, market research, and competitive analysis. Instead of manually monitoring job boards, you can automate the process to catch hiring momentum in real-time.

The [ATS Hiring Surge Monitor](https://apify.com/qualifyops/ats-hiring-surge-monitor) detects when companies post multiple job openings within short timeframes across popular applicant tracking systems. This signals business expansion, new initiatives, or seasonal growth—perfect indicators of companies with budget and growth plans.

The actor tracks hiring patterns, compares posting velocity, and flags sudden increases in job volume. You get structured data on which companies are ramping up teams, their open positions, and hiring timeline insights. This enables you to identify expansion-stage companies before they're fully committed to their growth initiatives.

Automate hiring surge detection to stay ahead of market opportunities without manual research.
